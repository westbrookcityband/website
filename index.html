About Us

The Westbrook City Band has been delighting audiences for over 140 years with a diverse musical repertoire. Composed of musicians from a variety of backgrounds, the band performs throughout the year enriching the cultural landscape of Westbrook, Maine.

As a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit organization, we rely on the support of our community to continue providing free concerts throughout the year.
